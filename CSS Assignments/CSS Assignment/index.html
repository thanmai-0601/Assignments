About HartFord India

Hartford India is the India-based technology and services arm of The Hartford, a leading global insurance and financial services company. It plays a key role in supporting The Hartford’s worldwide operations by delivering innovative technology solutions, data analytics, and digital platforms. Hartford India works on building and maintaining scalable applications, modernizing legacy systems, and enabling secure, data-driven decision-making across insurance functions.



With a strong focus on innovation and excellence, Hartford India contributes to areas such as application development, cloud computing, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and quality engineering. The organization fosters a collaborative and learning-driven work culture, offering opportunities for professionals and interns to work on real-world enterprise projects while developing technical and problem-solving skills that align with global industry standards.
